Differentiable Physics-informed Graph Networks

Feb 01, 2023 in Paper Notes

Abstract

Differentiable Physics-informed Graph Networks (DPGN) incorporate implicit physics knowledge which is given from domain experts by informing it in latent space.

1 Introduction

There is a limited number of labeled examples or there is much noise in the data of natural phenomenon.

Incorporating domain knowledge has several benefits: 1. more stable and prevent overfitting; 2. provides theoretical guidance and helps training with less data; 3. more robust and easier to be extended.
